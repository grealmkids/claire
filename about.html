About Me

History

Claire Ochola, born 29th May 1996, is an artist who is not only gifted but also academically qualified. She has a bachelor's degree in Music from Makerere University and a certificate from Cheryl Porter Vocal coach. She has been working and improving her craft since 2015 when she first joined university. She has written a number of songs under different topics like faith, hope, love and education. She composed her first song when she was 12 years old. She composed the reading album for the G-realm studios. This album has helped many children master the basics of reading.

She is also a creative writer who has written stories for children. Her history in writing started way back when she was only 9 years of age in primary school. She has gone on to write articles, play and movie scripts. Having experience with children has made her stories enjoyable to even adults.

She started doing poetry in 2018. Having rapped since 2013, she was encouraged to try poetry by one of her instructors Pastor Elizabeth Naluwembe. She tried for the first time in a Sunday service and she has never looked back. She has graced weddings with her unique spoken word poetry and made the day special for not only the bride and groom, but also the guests. She has also written poems for birthdays. The sky is the limit, only God knows how far she can go when it comes to creativity.
